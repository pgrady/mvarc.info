Mt. Vernon Amateur Radio Club Volunteer Examiners

We administer amateur radio examinations monthly near downtown Alexandria, Virginia.

About our exams:
  • All of our tests are "walk-in" - we don't do pre-registration.
  • We provide all necessary paperwork. Do not bring your own FCC Form 605-you must use the form we provide - has pre-printed info.
  • All our exams are FREE.
Bring to the test:
  • Picture ID issued by a Government agency
  • FCC FRN number --OR-- your Social Security Number. We can use your SSN only if the FCC has NEVER issued an FRN to you. If you are currently licensed you will find your FRN on your license.
  • If you do not have an FRN, we STRONGLY RECOMMEND that you obtain an FRN from the FCC at https://apps.fcc.gov/coresWeb/publicHome.do
  • Pens/Pencils. We do ask your to fill your forms with blue or black ink. You may bring your own calculator. If you plan on using your own calculator, be prepared to demonstrate that all memories are empty.
Prepare for your test:
  • The testing pool is published. See http://www.arrl.org/question-pools
  • A number of internet sites have online practice tests. Try searching for "amateur radio exam practice" in your favorite search engine
  • Good luck
